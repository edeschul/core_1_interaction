Why are baths so cozy on a rainy day? I always enjoy taking a nice warm bath on a rainy day. I like to fill the bathtub up with very warm water and add bubble soap. Outside, it might be raining and cold and wet, but inside, you can turn the cold rain water into a day of warm, bath-soaking, relaxtion.

I always enjoy a bath with bubbles. It makes it more fun and it adds a nice aroma.

You can also use a bath bomb as well. I especially like the bath bombs that dye the water a pretty color.

Optional is to have something to entertain yourself in the bath. I like to read a book or sometimes watch a show (just be careful keeping your electronics away from the water).

If you would like to make your bath cozy, I would recommend lighting some candles.
